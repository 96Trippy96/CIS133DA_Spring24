MOSFETS Overview

What is a MOSFET?

A MOSFET (Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Field-Effect Transistor) is a type of electronic switch used to control electrical current flow in devices.

What do they do?

MOSFETs can control electricity flow quickly and efficiently in various devices, acting like a gate that opens or closes.
